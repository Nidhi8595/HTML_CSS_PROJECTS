About College

Gautam Buddha University is a university established by the Uttar Pradesh Gautam Buddha University Act 2002 and came into existence in 2008. It is approved by University Grants Commission under section 12-B and accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council with B+ grade.The University offers various undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in the domains of Arts, Commerce, Science, Engineering, Management, Computer Applications, Biotechnology, Buddhist studies, and so on. The University provides experienced faculty members who have a bulk of knowledge because they are highly qualified. Most of them have a Ph.D. degree. They have good knowledge of their subjects, both practical and theoretical. Teachers are very helpful, and they are always available to help their students.

Gautam buddha university facilities

  • Library: The university has a Central Library with about 47200+ books and a seating capacity of about 2000 students at a time. The library is well-resourced with journals, magazines, ebooks, and books.
  • Recreational Activities: A swimming pool, gymnasium, and other activities are available for students. They have a separate gymnasium for boys and girls.
  • Wi-Fi campus: The entire campus of GBU is connected with a strong wifi network.
  • Central Computer Center: The university has well-equipped modernized computer labs with the latest technology and software.
  • Hostel: There are 18 hostels for boys and girls with proper hygiene and safety.
  • Sports: Students can play both indoor and outdoor games on the campus. They have a big playground for outdoor games. Lawn tennis courts, badminton courts, and volleyball courts are also available.
  • Auditorium and Conference Room: The university has 11 auditoriums with a capacity of 3000 students, out of which 9 are IT-enabled auditoriums. There are 16 conference rooms for organizing workshops, seminars, events, etc.
  • Medical Centre: Medical facilities are available to the students and staff. Doctors and nurses are available in the medical center.
